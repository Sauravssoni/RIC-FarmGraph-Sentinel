## Solution title

FarmGraph Rakshak — Rajasthan’s Offline Crop-Health and Predictive Outbreak-Response Grid

## Challenge / description — 435 characters

Rajasthan’s crop-health response is slowed by poor field evidence, weak connectivity, fragmented expert review and manual coordination. FarmGraph Rakshak is an offline-first crop-health operations platform for bajra, mustard, guar and cumin. It checks image quality, preserves image/voice evidence, routes uncertain cases to experts and KVKs, detects clusters, forecasts 72-hour workload and ranks field actions with full auditability.

## Value proposition — 415 characters

FarmGraph reduces officer and expert overload by automatically ranking cases, forecasting review demand, detecting rising districts, protecting KVK SLAs and batching field missions. Farmers gain an offline Hindi-first evidence workflow; government teams gain traceable cases, Farm Digital Twins, safe advisories, outcome tracking and integration-ready adapters for IMD, Bhashini, AGMARKNET, Raj Kisan and AgriStack.

## Envision / future vision — 442 characters

We envision a Rajasthan crop-health intelligence grid where every field report remains usable offline, every consequential decision stays expert-controlled and every verified case strengthens local response capacity. A 90-day pilot across Jodhpur, Nagaur and Jalore will validate evidence quality, expert turnaround, KVK response, short-horizon workload forecasting, safe advisories and the governed creation of Rajasthan-labelled model data.

## Unique selling point — 439 characters

Unlike classifier apps that stop at a disease label, FarmGraph closes the full evidence-to-response loop: image-quality rejection, offline image/voice evidence, uncertainty and abstention, expert verification, Farm Digital Twins, explainable outbreak scoring, 72-hour operations forecasting, nearest-KVK referral, mission batching, governed advisories, outcomes and consent-aware learning—without fabricating accuracy or government access.

Funds will cover field deployment, agronomy/KVK participation, Rajasthan-labelled evidence collection, model evaluation, Hindi/regional accessibility, government integration workshops, security, independent impact measurement and programme handover. ## One-line solution

An offline-first crop-health operating system that captures trustworthy field evidence, forecasts government workload, keeps experts in control, coordinates KVK and field response and learns safely from verified outcomes.

## Problem identified and solution proposed — under 100 words

Rajasthan’s crop-health response is fragmented: poor images, unreliable connectivity, unstructured expert escalation, manually tracked KVK referrals and isolated field reports delay action. FarmGraph Rakshak provides an offline Hindi-first PWA for image and voice evidence, quality checks, uncertainty-aware screening, expert verification, Farm Digital Twins, outbreak clustering, 72-hour workload forecasting, ranked next-best actions, representative missions, nearest-KVK referral, safe advisories and outcome tracking. It reduces repetitive coordination while retaining human control and truthful government-data states.

## Innovation / uniqueness — under 100 words

FarmGraph is not another disease-label app. It joins Evidence Quality Before AI, offline image/voice evidence, transparent abstention, expert-controlled decisions, Farm Digital Twins, explainable outbreak scoring, short-horizon operational forecasting, rising-district and KVK SLA alerts, representative mission batching, privacy-masked referral packs, governed advisories and consent-aware learning. Every recommendation shows its evidence and assumptions. Government adapters are labelled live, cached, public-directory, credentials-required or awaiting authority, preventing fabricated integrations or impact claims.

## Why it reduces manual overload

- Officers receive a ranked action list instead of scanning every case.
- Experts receive a structured queue ranked by spread risk, uncertainty and evidence quality.
- District trends and 72-hour demand are calculated automatically.
- KVK deadlines are monitored instead of maintained in separate spreadsheets.
- Representative missions batch verification instead of visiting every report.
- Evidence and referral packs are generated from the case record.
- Operator-time assumptions are disclosed and will be validated through a pilot time-and-motion study.
